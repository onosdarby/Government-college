HISTORY

Government College Ughelli was founded in 1945. It was sited initially at Warri and was known as Warri Middle School, before it was later moved to Ughelli. The First Students (Mariners) walked into their classrooms on January 19, 1945. Modeled after its remarkable “Public Schools” contemporaries in Britain, Government College Ughelli was conceived as an institution where students were brought up to appreciate the virtues of manliness, sportsmanship, intellectual and moral excellence, integrity and of a clean and salubrious environment. However, especial care was taken to ensure that the character of the student was molded without their personality being distorted.

MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL

Dear Students, Parents, Alumni, and Friends,

Welcome to the online home of Government College Ughelli. It is with great pride and excitement that I extend a warm welcome to you all on behalf of the entire GCU community.

At Government College Ughelli, we are dedicated to providing an exceptional educational experience that combines academic excellence with a wide range of extracurricular activities. Our mission is to cultivate an environment where students can develop intellectually, socially, and emotionally, preparing them to become responsible and successful citizens in an ever-changing world.

We are proud of our rich history and the achievements of our alumni, many of whom have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields around the globe. Our tradition of excellence is built on the dedication of our skilled faculty, the enthusiasm of our students, and the unwavering support of our alumni and parents.

We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation in our educational approach, and we encourage you to explore our site to learn more about what makes Government College Ughelli a unique and vibrant learning community.

Thank you for visiting our website. We look forward to your involvement and support as we continue to build on our legacy of excellence.

Warm regards,

Principal, Government College Ughelli
